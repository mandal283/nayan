The huge asteroid '2001 FO32' will fly very close to the earth on Sunday. Researchers believe that this piece of rock formed in the early days of our solar system will give astronomers a rare opportunity to learn about it. The news agency AFP reported this information. Astronomers say that even if the asteroid is very close to the Earth, it is less likely to collide with the Earth. There is no such fear in the next few hundred years. Researchers at the US space agency NASA have assured that there is nothing to worry about the asteroid named '2001 FO32'. It will cross the earth from a safe distance on March 21.
